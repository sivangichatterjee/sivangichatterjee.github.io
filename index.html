Research

LLM-Based Agentic Debugging and Self-Correction

Cognitive Computation Group, University of Pennsylvania · Advised by Prof. Dan Roth, Mentored by Haoyu Wang (PhD) · Jan 2026 - Present

My current research investigates how large language models behave when they encounter errors in multi-step agentic pipelines. I am building trajectory-level datasets by introducing controlled perturbations into intermediate agent steps, studying how models detect and recover from mistakes, and exploring whether fine-tuning can improve their self-correction capabilities in complex software engineering tasks.
